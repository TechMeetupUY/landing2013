techMeetup UY is an annual conference created to and for Uruguayan developers.

It has 3 main objectives:

  • Empower local communities.
  • Encourage IT students.
  • Offering a day of professional actualization and networking of the highest quality.

It is addressed to all the Uruguayan IT sector: CTOs, IT Managers, IT responsibles, students and all IT professionals.

Present your talk

Do you have something interesting to say? With 30 minutes to showcase your ideas, this will be a very intense and dynamic event. Get ready to show what interests you the most! Tell us who you are, what you do and why your talk is important.

Everyone has the opportunity to present themselves equally, because we understand that without new ideas there is no development.
